What is Sophos Home?

Sophos Home delivers enterprise-level security for home PCs and Macs, providing protection against ransomware, malware, and hacking attempts.

What is the difference between Trial and Premium?

Premium: Real-time protection, remote management, 10 devices max.

Trial: Same features, but 30 days only + 3 devices.

What type of security measures does Sophos Home provide?

Includes AI detection, behavior analysis, web scanning, phishing protection.

Can I use Sophos Home along other antivirus software?

Not recommended. It may cause conflicts and reduce security.
