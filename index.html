What is Sophos Home?

Sophos Home delivers enterprise-level protection against malware, ransomware, and hacking attempts.

Difference between Trial and Premium

Premium: Real-time protection, remote management, up to 10 devices.

Trial: Same features, but limited to 30 days and 3 devices.

What security measures does it provide?

AI detection, behavioral analysis, phishing defense, exploit prevention, and web scanning.

Can I use it with other antivirus?

No. Running two antiviruses can conflict and weaken protection.
